# BOARD_MEMBER Handbook

BOARD_MEMBER has elevated community access compared with parent/family roles.

## Primary Responsibilities

- Support ADMIN with community operations.
- Help maintain child and community records.
- Publish timely community updates.

## What You Can Do

- View and manage your own community details (address/contact scope).
- Manage community board members.
- Create/update/delete posts in your community.
- Create/update/delete lectures and attendance records.
- Coordinate with admins on child updates and parent communication.

## What You Cannot Do

- Create/update/delete platform users.
- Assign admins to communities.
- Manage lessons catalog.
- Perform cross-community privileged operations.
- Create/update/inactivate children (admin-only workflow).

## Recommended Workflow

1. Review community profile data and keep address/contact details current.
2. Keep board-member assignments accurate.
3. Coordinate with ADMIN on child and attendance updates.
4. Publish posts for relevant announcements.
5. Escalate role or user-management requests to ADMIN/SUPER_ADMIN.
